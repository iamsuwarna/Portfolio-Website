Research

What is the concept of research?


Definition: Research is characterized as a methodical examination of a specific concern or issue using empirical methods. “Research is a structured investigation to classify, understand, forecast, and monitor the observed phenomenon,” according to American sociologist Earl Robert Babbie. It employs both inductive and deductive reasoning.”
